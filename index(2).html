TO MY POKÉBALL

We started as friends.

We didn't spend much time together.

Then somehow...

We made a contract.

And with that contract, I became your wife. You became my husband.

After that, we spent more time together. We laughed. We teased each other. We created memories I never thought a game could give me.

Then our promise became something even bigger.

✦ VOW UNLOCKED: "FOREVER AND ALWAYS." ✦

You once told me,

"Just because we started from the game doesn't mean we'll end from the game."

Those words made me believe that what we have is real. Not because of where we met. But because of who we became together.

Then there was the day you replied to one of my poems. You wrote:

"You know, as I get through more of these poems, the more I don't want to write how I feel about them. Because my response will always be broken down into the same thing. I want you. I don't want to read about some life where I can't have you. Or some story where our paths split once again. I just want you. And I don't even care if I can or not. You told me to get greedy. To use you whenever I need. And I will. So this will be my last and final message about these poems. And then, you can have me. All of me. For however long you want."

I don't think you realize what those words did to me.

They weren't just a reply to a poem. They became a promise I carry with me.

Whenever I doubt myself, I remember, "I just want you."

Whenever I'm afraid of losing you, I remember, "You can have me. All of me."

And whenever I wonder if this is all just a dream, I remember that you chose us.

Tonight, as the fireworks light up the sky, I don't wish for anything more. Because somehow, through one simple contract, I found the person I want to keep choosing.

My husband. My PokéBall.
